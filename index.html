About

JR Agency is a design studio specializing in web and graphic design, delivering creative solutions to elevate your brand.

  • 2022

    The Beginning of JR Agency

    JR Agency started with a simple vision: to craft functional and aesthetic web and graphic designs for startups and small businesses. It was a year of learning, experimenting, and laying the foundation for the agency's future.

  • Early 2023

    First Steps in the Industry

    The agency secured its first few clients, designing websites and branding materials that showcased creativity and attention to detail. This was the beginning of building a portfolio and gaining valuable experience.

  • Late 2023

    Growth and Recognition

    By the end of 2023, JR Agency had started gaining recognition in the local community. Referrals and positive reviews brought in new clients, allowing the agency to take on slightly bigger projects.

  • 2024

    Laying the Groundwork for the Future

    This year marks the expansion of JR Agency's capabilities, with a focus on refining skills and exploring new trends. The goal is to set a strong foundation for future growth while maintaining the quality and personal touch that clients love.
